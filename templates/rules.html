Game Rules

Welcome to the "Bunker" game! Here are the rules:

Objective

The main objective of the game is to survive in the bunker until the end and be the last remaining player.

Game Preparation

The game starts with each player entering the bunker. The room owner can start the game when there are enough players in the bunker.

Characteristics

Each player is assigned various characteristics such as age, height, weight, health status, phobia, hobby, personality, belongings, additional information, and knowledge. These characteristics play a key role in the game.

Turns

The game is played in turns. During each turn, players reveal one of their characteristics to others. After all players have revealed their characteristics, the voting phase begins.

Voting

Players vote for the player they wish to exclude from the game.
